Focaccia with Caramelized Onion, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds whit this festive focaccia appertizer topped whit sweet caramelized onions, tart cramberries, and creamy brie.
It will become your favourite holiday starter!

Ingredients

    For the focaccia:
  • 4 coups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teapons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teapons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
    For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desidered, thought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh of frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough
     In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
